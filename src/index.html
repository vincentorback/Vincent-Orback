Hyper Human is a new exhibition at the National Museum of Science and Technology in Stockholm.

Visitors will be challenged by a range of ethical questions about technical and medical development at three stations that I helped develop in collaboration with Spektra.

The graphical effects I developed for the transparent touch screens were made using WebGL with PixiJS as a framework.

Front-end developer

Välj Att Sluta (Choose to stop) is a helpline offering advice to those who want help to change a controlling or violent behavior. Callers talk anonymously to a therapist or other professional who then suggest further treatment.

I helped by building the interface used by the operators taking calls.

Developer, designer

Författaren is a magazine for members of The Swedish Writers’ Union. Författaren monitors author conditions both inside and outside of the country, copyright, freedom of the press, cultural policy, book industry and library issues.

I designed the website heavily inspired by the print magazine's design and then also built it using WordPress.

Developer, designer

A bit about me

I like building solid web projects. Things that last, things that solve real problems that need solving. I like my websites to be loading fast and the pace we travel the internet to be slow.

The tools I use are a mix of different technologies including React, Node, WordPress and more.

Currently working as a web developer at Spektra.
